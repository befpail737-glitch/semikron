Technical Capabilities

Our expertise in power electronics design and implementation

Application Engineering

Our FAE team provides comprehensive support from initial design to production implementation. We offer guidance on component selection, circuit design, thermal management, and system optimization. Our engineers have decades of combined experience in power electronics design across all major application areas.

System Design

We assist with complete system design including power modules, gate drivers, thermal management, and protection circuits. Our design services cover all power levels from low-power IPM applications to high-power industrial systems.

Testing & Validation

Our technical center provides testing and validation services to verify design performance and reliability. We offer thermal characterization, electrical validation, and failure analysis services to support our customers' development processes.

Training & Education

We provide technical training on power semiconductor technologies, application-specific implementation, and design best practices. Our training programs are tailored to customer needs and application requirements.

Inventory and Supply Chain

Ensuring continuous availability of Semikron products

Extensive Stock

We maintain one of the most comprehensive inventories of Semikron products in the region, including both current production items and discontinued parts to ensure continuity of supply for existing designs. Our inventory management system provides real-time visibility into stock levels and availability.

Our warehouse facilities are equipped with proper environmental controls and handling procedures to maintain product quality. We implement FIFO (First In, First Out) inventory rotation to ensure product freshness and reliability.

Supply Chain Excellence

Our direct partnership with Semikron headquarters ensures preferential allocation of products during supply tightness. We work closely with Semikron's planning and logistics teams to ensure adequate inventory for customer needs.

We provide comprehensive supply chain services including forecast management, consignment inventory, and JIT delivery to support our customers' manufacturing requirements.
