Frequently Asked Questions About Semikron Distribution

Get answers to common questions about our authorized distribution services

What is an authorized Semikron distributor?

An authorized Semikron distributor is officially certified by Semikron to sell genuine products with full factory warranty and technical support. LiTong Group is a recognized authorized distributor with direct supply chain access to Semikron's manufacturing facilities in Germany and worldwide.

How fast can you deliver Semikron products?

With local warehouses in USA, Germany, India, Japan, Korea, and 8 other countries, we offer 24-48 hour delivery to major industrial centers. Same-day shipping is available for stocked items ordered before 2 PM local time. Our Asia Pacific network provides 1-3 day standard delivery to China, Japan, and Korea.

Do you provide technical support for Semikron products?

Yes, our factory-trained Field Application Engineers (FAEs) provide comprehensive support including system design consultation, thermal analysis, gate driver selection, and application guidance for all Semikron power modules, IPMs, and drivers.

Can you supply discontinued Semikron parts?

Yes, we maintain strategic inventory of discontinued and end-of-life Semikron products including SKiP 3/4 systems, legacy SEMIPACK modules, and older SEMITRANS generations to support long-term industrial applications and prevent production line disruptions.

How do I verify you are an authorized distributor?

You can verify our authorization by visiting the official Semikron distributor locator or viewing our authorization certificates. We provide full traceability documentation with every order.

What industries do you serve?

We serve industrial automation, renewable energy (solar & wind), electric vehicles, UPS systems, motor drives, and power supply manufacturers. Our technical team has deep expertise in each application area.
