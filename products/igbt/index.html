FAE Technical Commentary

Expert insights on IGBT module selection

For high-efficiency applications, we recommend the latest SEMiX series with IGBT4 technology. The reduced VCEsat and optimized switching characteristics result in significantly lower conduction and switching losses, especially beneficial for renewable energy inverters where efficiency directly impacts ROI.

When space is at a premium, the eMPack series provides the highest power density with its revolutionary pin-fin baseplate design. The integrated cooling solution eliminates the need for thermal interface materials and reduces the overall system footprint.

For high-temperature applications, older Trench technology modules may be preferred over newer Field-Stop designs due to their superior temperature coefficients. Our application engineers can help determine the optimal technology for your specific operating conditions.
