FAE Technical Commentary

Expert insights on Semikron product selection

For compact designs, MiniSKiiP's copper-less spring-contact technology provides the best cost optimization while maintaining excellent thermal performance. The technology eliminates the need for copper base plates, reducing both weight and thermal resistance.

When designing for high-vibration environments like wind turbines, SKiiP systems with their press-fit technology provide superior mechanical reliability compared to traditional soldered connections.

For high-efficiency applications, the latest IGBT4 technology in SEMiX series provides excellent switching characteristics with reduced conduction losses compared to earlier generations.
