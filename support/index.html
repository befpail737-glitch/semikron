FAE Technical Commentary

Expert insights on common technical challenges

For thermal design challenges, many designers underestimate the impact of thermal interface materials and mounting pressure. Always follow Semikron's recommended torque values and use thermal grease with verified conductivity values.

When selecting gate drivers, consider not only the drive requirements but also the protection features. Modern drivers with desaturation detection and active Miller clamping can significantly improve system reliability.

For reliability-critical applications, implement proper derating of power semiconductors. Using components at 80% of their rated capacity can double their operational lifetime compared to full-rated usage.
